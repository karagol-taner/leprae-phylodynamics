The raw genomic sequencing data analyzed in this study were previously published and are publicly accessible through the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI) Sequence Read Archive (SRA) and the European Nucleotide Archive (ENA). The 11th-century medieval Winchester human sequences are available under BioProject PRJNA200950 (NCBI). The contemporary British red squirrel (Sciurus vulgaris) sequences, encompassing both the Mycobacterium leprae and Mycobacterium lepromatosis isolates, are available under BioProject PRJNA325727 (ENA). The modern global clinical human isolates are available under BioProject PRJNA317287 (NCBI).
